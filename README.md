# Diamond-Matrix

This solution uses a matrix to draw a diamond shape of any size on a matrix of length n, where the n is always an odd number

#### [Example 1]

> Drawing a diamond shape on a matrix plane of size: 13 and a diamond size of 5.

            *
          * * *
        * * * * *
      * * * * * * *       
    * * * * * * * * *     
      * * * * * * *       
        * * * * *
          * * *
            *
